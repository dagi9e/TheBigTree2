The Big Tree

Once there was a Big Tree in the fields of Papua New Guinea. It was very tall and almost touched the sky. Birds lived in it, insects lived in it, squarels and walabies too. It was a tree that all animals big and small could come and rest under. But one day, man said he wanted to build a house. He looked and looked and his eyes rested on the big tree. However, he also saw that other creatures already made a house in that tree. Now he was faced with two choices;

  1. Cut the tree

  2. Leave the tree for the animals.
